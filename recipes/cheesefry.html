CheeseFry

Description

a dish of delicious cheese fry's.

we need potato, pizza cheese and oil

fry the potato in oil then melt cheese.

add melted cheese on the fried potato mmmmm

Ingredients

  • potato
  • oil
  • pizza cheese

Steps

  1. peel the potato
  2. cut the potato in slim slices
  3. make the oil boil
  4. pour the potatoes into the oil
  5. after potatoes fried good, dry them of oil
  6. pour potatoes into a separate plat than the oiled one
  7. pour cheeses on the potatoes
  8. put it into microwave
  9. let the degrees on 40c
  10. bring them out after ten minute
